Orientation Briefing

MIMEOGRAPHED — DO NOT POST ON CAMPUS BULLETIN BOARDS

It is February 1960. Three years ago, seven young people sat down at the Royal Ice Cream parlor and were arrested for it. Last week, Greensboro students sat down at a lunch counter and would not leave. The moment has arrived in Durham.

You chair the coordinating committee. You have fourteen weeks before the semester ends. Break the merchants' resolve before the calendar breaks your campaign.

Discipline is not a slogan. It is the strategy. Train your people. Mind the bail fund. Keep Hayti behind you. And know your volunteers by name — you will be spending their courage.
