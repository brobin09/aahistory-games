ABOUT THE PROJECT

About These Games

These games are built to make African American history playable, discussable, and hard to forget. Each one is designed to teach a specific historical skill or truth — source criticism, campaign logistics, the economics of Black enterprise, the calculus of escape and mobility — through mechanics rather than memorization.

All games run in the browser on any device. Nothing to install. Classroom use is warmly encouraged, and each game is designed to open a discussion, not close one.
