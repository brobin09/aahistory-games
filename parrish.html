WHO YOU ARE

You sit on the boards of Black Durham's institutions across seventy-two years, from the founding of N.C. Mutual in 1898 to the closing of the books in 1970.

HOW A SEASON PLAYS

History moves first — an event arrives, sometimes with a decision, sometimes with a document to read like a historian. Then the board acts — you pick one initiative. Everything is written into the Ledger on the right.

WHAT THE NUMBERS MEAN

CAPITAL — money to build with. Institutions earn it every season.
TRUST — the community's faith in the Mile. It drains a little every season; institutions and good choices refill it.
DEPENDENCE — how much you owe white Durham's goodwill. Cheap now, expensive later.
ARCHIVE — the historical record you build by reading documents well.
THE GOAL — reach 1970 with the highest Legacy: institutions standing, trust held, archive full, dependence low. Build early. The final act is coming, and it cannot be stopped — only answered.
